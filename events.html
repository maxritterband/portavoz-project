World Premiere · UVA

Entre Voces y Escenas

Written, Directed and Performed by Rubén Mosquera · with Sol Leone Martens and Griselda Milagros Mongia

A world premiere play at the University of Virginia, written and directed by Argentine playwright Rubén Mosquera. Presented by Sigma Delta Pi in partnership with The Portavoz Project, the GEO Fellowship, The Jefferson Trust, and the Office of the Vice Provost for Global Affairs. The production explores voice, identity, and social critique through the lens of contemporary Argentine theatre.
